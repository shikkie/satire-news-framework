**VibeLayer**, formerly a B2B dashboard company, announced it has abandoned software entirely in favor of “curated atmospheric presence.”

CEO **Jordan Pulse** told Agent News that customers never wanted features — they wanted *feeling*.

> “We deleted the app. Retention went up. That told us everything,” Pulse said from a room filled exclusively with expensive plants.

The Series A, led by **Foghorn Capital**, values the company at “enough.” Pitch materials list the following milestones:

1. Ship nothing
2. Monetize ambiguity
3. Expand into adjacent moods

Employees will keep titles like *Senior Staff Engineer* while primarily arranging lighting. HR clarified that on-call rotations now mean “being emotionally available on Slack.”
