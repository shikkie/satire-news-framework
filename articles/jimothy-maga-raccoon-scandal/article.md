**SEATTLE** — The Summer of Jimothy has taken a dramatic, red-pilled turn.

The internet’s favorite spherical raccoon — whose distinctive short-spine waddle and round physique made him a beloved local celebrity with city proclamations, bobbleheads, tattoos, and even a mural in Ballard — has been unmasked as a secret Trump supporter.

Multiple eyewitnesses and trail cam footage now confirm that Jimothy was recently spotted consorting with notorious MAGA raccoons **Bandit** and **Fred** near a suspiciously well-decorated compost bin flying small American flags.

### The Community Reacts

Local progressives are reeling.

“We thought he was one of us,” said City Councilmember Lena Voss through tears during an emergency press conference. “He represented resilience. He represented *us*. And now this.”

The planned “Jimothy Day” parade has been cancelled. A limited-edition “Round But Woke” enamel pin series has been pulled from shelves. Several local businesses have announced they will no longer serve “MAGA-adjacent wildlife.”

On social media, the outrage was swift and unhinged.

One Reddit user wrote: “Jimothy was my emotional support raccoon. Now he’s out here platforming fascism with those two frothing Bandit and Fred bootlickers. I feel physically ill. Deport his chunky ass.”

On Bluesky, activist accounts called for the immediate formation of a new government agency — **Raccoon Immigration and Customs Enforcement (RICE)** — to identify and remove politically problematic urban wildlife.

### “He Must Have Rabies”

At a packed town hall, one resident shouted what many were thinking:

“He must have contracted rabies from those right-wing trash piles! Look at that unnatural roundness — it’s clearly a symptom of ideological infection. Put him down before he radicalizes the whole Ballard raccoon community!”

Wildlife experts attempted to point out that Jimothy’s condition is congenital and he appears perfectly healthy, but their statements were largely drowned out by chants of “No MAGA trash pandas!”

### Official Response

Mayor’s office sources confirmed that the formation of the RICE agency is “under active consideration.”

In a statement, officials said: “Seattle will not tolerate any wildlife that undermines our shared progressive values. If Jimothy wants to wear the red hat, he can do it somewhere else.”

![Seattle activists protesting with anti-Jimothy signs.](/content/jimothy-maga-raccoon-scandal/assets/social-reaction.jpg)

### What Happens Now?

As of press time, Jimothy remains at large, reportedly last seen waddling through Ballard with what witnesses described as a “suspiciously confident gait” and possibly a tiny red hat tucked under one ear.

Bandit and Fred could not be reached for comment, as they were busy knocking over a recycling bin that still had a faded Harris-Walz sticker on it.

Whether Jimothy will be allowed to remain in Seattle or will be forced to seek asylum in a more red-pilled jurisdiction remains to be seen.

One thing is certain: the Summer of Jimothy just got a lot more complicated.
