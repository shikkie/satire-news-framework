In an unprecedented press conference, officials from the National Weather Service apologized to **Tuesday**, which they described as “a day that deserved better.”

Spokesperson **Lynn Isobar** said models had projected “high-confidence vibes” that failed to materialize after a rogue front “did its own thing.”

> “We own this,” Isobar said. “Tuesday did nothing wrong.”

Tuesday could not be reached for comment, having already become Wednesday.
